## Step 2 — Score Each Role
Rate each role from 1–5.

### Scoring Criteria
- **Repetition** — How often does this task happen?
- **Clarity** — How clearly can you define the work?
- **Frequency** — Does it happen often enough to matter?
- **Business Value** — Would solving this create real leverage?
- **Risk** — How safe is it if the AI gets it partially wrong?
- **Reviewability** — Can a human check the output quickly?
